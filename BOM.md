# Bill of Materials (BOM): Syringe Extruder

This BOM lists the mechanical and electronic components required to assemble the custom syringe extruder for use with the Arctos robotic arm 3D printing module.

| Item | Description | Quantity | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Syringe** | 60ml Plastic Syringe | 1 | Main reservoir for material deposition |
| **Threaded Rod** | M5 x 100mm Threaded Rod | 1 | Translates stepper rotation into linear plunger motion |
| **Nut** | M5 Stainless Steel Hex Nut | 1 | Acts as the leadscrew nut |
| **Stepper Motor** | 28BYJ-48 Stepper Motor (5V or 12V) | 1 | High-torque geared stepper for extrusion control |
| **Bolts** | M3 x 10mm Socket Head Bolts | 4 | Mounting for the stepper motor and frame |

### Assembly Notes:
1.  **Threaded Rod Alignment**: Ensure the M5 rod is perfectly centered in the 28BYJ stepper coupler to prevent wobbling during extrusion.
2.  **Nut Seating**: The M5 nut should be securely pressed into the 3D-printed plunger interface.
3.  **Bolts**: Use washers with the M3x10 bolts to distribute the load on the plastic mounting tabs.
